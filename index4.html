Artificial Intelligence

Generative AI

Definition

Generative AI uses advanced algorithms to create new content from existing data. For example, it can generate high-quality images, music, text, and videos.

  • Text models: GPT generates coherent content, such as summaries, articles, or scripts.
  • Image models: DALL-E creates images based on textual descriptions, combining unique concepts.
  • Audio and video: Systems like Jukebox generate original music, and deepfake tools allow the creation of realistic videos of nonexistent people.

Impact

Creative industry: Democratizes content creation, allowing people without technical experience to generate graphics, stories, or designs.

Automation: Simplifies complex tasks, such as generating architectural plans or product design.

Fun Facts

Musical deepfake: In 2020, an AI generated songs by imitating the voices of deceased artists, such as Elvis Presley and Amy Winehouse.

AI Dungeon: It is a text-based role-playing game completely generated by AI, allowing for personalized adventures.

Video game creation : AI tools already generate levels and entire worlds for games automatically.
